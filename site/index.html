Project Athena

A reproducible quant research platform for discovering and validating systematic trading edges — built on $0 budget, free-tier data, and a local database. Not a trading bot, and not (yet) anything that touches real money.

What we want to achieve

Build a lean, reliable research platform utilizing $0 budget, free-tier data sources, and a local database architecture — that can eventually support AI-assisted and multi-agent research on a distributed stack.

Where we are right now

  1. Project Foundation — governance docs, directory layout, toolchainDone
  2. Data Ingestion & Data Quality — Binance & Bybit adapters, TimescaleDB storageIn Progress
  3. Backtesting Engine ↔ Feature Engineering ↔ Strategy ResearchPending
  4. Experiment TrackingPending
  5. Paper Trading (simulated execution on live data)Pending
  6. AI Research Assistant & Multi-Agent DebatePending
  7. Live Trading ControlsPending

Hard constraint

  • No live trades until research, validation, paper trading, risk controls, and human approval are mature.
  • AI assists research — it never sizes positions, places trades, or bypasses risk controls.
  • Every backtest is treated skeptically: lookahead bias, overfitting, and unrealistic fills are assumed until ruled out.
